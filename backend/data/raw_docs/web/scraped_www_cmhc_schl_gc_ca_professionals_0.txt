# Solving housing affordability together

Affordable housing for all is an ambitious goal, and we can’t do it alone. We
offer funding programs, market intelligence, research, data and a team
dedicated to push the boundaries of housing innovation. Let’s work together to
find new solutions.

  * [ Project funding and mortgage financing  Access our project funding, mortgage loan insurance and securitization programs.  ](/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing)
  * [ Housing markets, data and research  Understand Canada’s housing industry using market reports, research and data.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research)
  * [ Industry innovation and leadership  Access funding and expertise to drive housing innovation and accelerate supply.  ](/professionals/industry-innovation-and-leadership)
  * [ Events and speakers  Join us at an industry events or request a speaker for your next conference.  ](/professionals/events-and-speakers)
