# Housing innovation

Building and renovating affordable, quality housing faces many challenges –
rising costs is just one example. We are working with the entire housing
production ecosystem to collaborate and create innovative solutions to
overcome challenges and build a better Canada in the process. Learn more about
the opportunities within Housing Innovation.

  * [ Longitudinal Outcomes Research Grant  Guiding evidence-based decision-making for vulnerable populations through research.  ](/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/longitudinal-outcomes-research)
  * [ National Housing Strategy Solutions Labs  Tackling complex housing issues to enable potential solutions  ](/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/solution-labs)
  * [ NHS Demonstrations Initiative  Demonstrating innovative practices, technologies, programs, policies and strategies  ](/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/nhs-demonstrations-initiative)
  * [ Housing Supply Challenge  $300 million in funding to break down barriers affecting housing supply.  ](/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/housing-supply-challenge)
  * [ Affordable Housing Innovation Fund  Loans, forgivable loans, contributions and financing options that support housing innovation across the housing continuum.  ](/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/affordable-housing-innovation-fund)
  * [ Expert Community on Housing (ECoH)  Join the community working together to scale housing solutions.  ](/professionals/industry-innovation-and-leadership/industry-collaboration/expert-community-on-housing)
  * [ Collaborative Housing Research Network  Generating research, data and insights on housing in Canada.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/housing-research/collaborative-housing-research-network)
