###  [Bank of Canada lowers policy rate to
2¼%](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/2025/10/fad-press-release-2025-10-29/)

[Media Relations](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/press/contacts/) [Ottawa,
Ontario](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/search/?location\[\]=ottawa_ontario)

points to 2.25%, with the Bank Rate at 2.5% and the deposit rate at 2.20%.
