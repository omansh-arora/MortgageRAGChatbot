# Industry innovation and leadership

To innovate is to collaborate. We seek to mobilize the public and private
sectors re-imagining how we can make housing affordable for every Canadian. We
offer funding, resources and expertise to help accelerate the delivery of
affordable, sustainable, and socially inclusive housing supply.

  * [ Housing innovation  Working with the entire housing ecosystem to collaborate and co-create innovative solutions to overcome housing challenges and accelerate supply.  ](/professionals/industry-innovation-and-leadership/housing-innovation)
  * [ Our Partners  To ensure that everyone in Canada has access to safe, affordable and inclusive housing, we need to think differently and work collaboratively. We work with governments, non-profit organizations and private sector organizations, including corporations, social entrepreneurs, philanthropists and other partners to close the gap in housing needs.  ](/professionals/industry-innovation-and-leadership/our-partners)
  * [ Industry collaboration  Encouraging the exchange of knowledge to improve housing outcomes for everyone in Canada.  ](/professionals/industry-innovation-and-leadership/industry-collaboration)
  * [ Industry expertise  Access our pool of resources to expand your housing knowledge.  ](/professionals/industry-innovation-and-leadership/industry-expertise)
