# Housing markets

Get reliable, impartial and up-to-date housing market reports, analysis and
knowledge. Access housing, mortgage and rental market trends, forecasts and
insights at the local, provincial and national levels.

  * [ CMHC Reports Calendar  Find out when we’re releasing our latest housing reports.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/news-releases-reports-calendar)
  * [ Housing market reports  Discover the latest trends, forecasts and analysis on the housing market.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/market-reports/housing-market)
  * [ Mortgage market and consumer reports  Access reports covering the Canadian mortgage market and consumer behaviours.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/market-reports/mortgage-market)
  * [ Fall 2024 Rental Market Report  Explore detailed rental market insights from major Canadian cities.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/market-reports/rental-market-reports-major-centres)
