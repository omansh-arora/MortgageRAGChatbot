## Regular Data Releases

### [Monthly Housing Starts and Other Construction
Data](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/housing-data/data-
tables/housing-market-data/monthly-housing-starts-construction-data-tables)

Monthly release of statistics on housing starts in Canada, issued on the 11th
working day of the month.

#### 2025 release dates:

  * January 16 
  * February 17 
  * March 17
  * April 15 
  * May 15
  * June 16
  * July 16
  * August 18
  * September 16
  * October 16
  * November 18
  * December 15

### [Housing Information Monthly](/en/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-
research/housing-data/data-tables/housing-market-
data?guide=Housing%20construction%20activity)

Monthly release of more than 90 easy-to-read, data-rich statistical tables on
starts, completions, absorptions and prices for provincial and local markets.

### [Housing Market Information Portal](https://www03.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/hmip-
pimh/#Profile/1/1/Canada)

Free, easy-to-use access anytime to the latest housing market data for Canada.

[Sign-up to the Housing Research Newsletter](/cmhc-enewsletters) for the
latest research and insight updates.

If you have questions please [contact us](mailto:HminformationMH@cmhc-
schl.gc.ca).
