# Housing data: latest statistics and trends

CMHC’s housing data will keep you up-to-date with industry changes. Our data
tables provide the latest statistics on household characteristics, housing
indicators, housing and rental markets, mortgage and debt. We are leading the
charge to develop tools and applications to facilitate the exchange of data in
the industry and address data gaps.

  * [ Data tables  Get the most up-to-date information on rental markets and housing markets, household characteristics, finance, securitization and core housing need.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/housing-data/data-tables)
  * [ Housing market information portal  Access up-to-date Canadian housing data from the neighbourhood level to a national scale.  ](https://www.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/hmiportal)
  * [ Residential Mortgage Industry Data Dashboard  Find data and insights on the mortgage market in Canada.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/housing-data/residential-mortgage-industry-data-dashboard)
  * [ CMHC licence agreement for the use of data  Terms and conditions for your use of CMHC data and information.  ](/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/housing-data/cmhc-licence-agreement-use-of-data)
  * [ Housing Knowledge Centre  Our library specializes in housing literature, publications, statistics and more for your research needs.  ](/library)
